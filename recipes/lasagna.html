Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna traces its origins to ancient Greece, where the first known references to layered dishes resembling modern lasagna appear.

The Romans later adapted and refined these recipes, using layers of pasta, cheese, and sauce, similar to today's versions.

Steps

  1. This recipe uses store-bought sauce (make sure you get one you love).
  2. The lasagna noodles cook right in the sauce.
  3. The lasagna can (and should) rest before eating.
